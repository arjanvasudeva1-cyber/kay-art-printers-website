About Kay Art

A legacy pressed
into every sheet.

Kay Art Printers began in 1972 and has grown into one of North India’s pioneer specialists in customised packaging and commercial printing — serving customers across industry verticals, with a deep focus on pharmaceuticals.

What hasn’t changed is the philosophy: a symbiosis of the highest standards of printing technology, creative implementation and an unwavering commitment to quality. Keen attention to detail and precise execution have made Kay Art a preferred partner of choice — every job is checked, sorted and inspected before it leaves the floor.

Today, that means a 95-strong team, a six-press Heidelberg-led floor, in-house plate making, and a quality system built on ISO guidelines and GMP — all inside a 20,000 sq ft plant at Kundli, on Delhi’s doorstep.

✳ In-process quality checkpoints

01

Artwork & approval

URS and artwork arrive from the customer’s packaging or purchase team by e-mail. A PDF proof goes back for sign-off — and work begins only against approved artwork and a purchase order.

URS · soft-copy artwork · PDF approval · PO
02

Pre-press & plates

Text is verified against the approved artwork, positives are developed where required, and plates are made in-house on our CTP line under an Esko ArtPro+ workflow.

Esko ArtPro+ · Amsky Ausetter T832 CTP · IPV PDF proof check
03

Printing In-process control

Sheets run on Heidelberg offset presses with continuous checks on Pantone shades, text and registration through the run — followed by sheet sorting.

Heidelberg SM-74 · SORMZ · Perfection · Adast Dominant 715
04

Coating & die-cutting In-process control

Varnish or UV/aqua coating, then precision die-cutting on automatic platens with laser-made dies — and individual cartons sorted twice.

Offline UV/Aqua coater · Autoprint Dextra 80 ×2 · laser dies
05

Pasting & inspection In-process control

High-speed folder-gluers paste each carton before a 100% final visual inspection in a dedicated, lit inspection room — then bundle formation and wrapping.

DGM Smartfold 800PC · Vijaya folder-gluer · Fevicol (Pidilite)
06

Packing & dispatch Final QC

Poly-bagged, packed into fresh CFB shippers — taped and labelled — with a final quality check on every box, then dispatched with multi-ups, shade cards, COA and samples.

Packing list · COA · shade cards · retained samples
